Backup Your Wallet

A wallet backup protects your tezzies against computer or software failure. If you lose the backup and can't access your wallet for some reasons, your tezzies are permanently lost!

It is recommended that you store backups of both forms of your key (the Keystore file version and paper wallet version) in physically separate, offline environments.
